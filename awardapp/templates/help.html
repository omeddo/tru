Uploading my site

*To upload your site you need to first create an account.
*Afterwards you can navigate to home and upload your program via the submit site button.
*In the project submit form there are required fields to be filled, Project category being one of them.
In this field you need to input the category your project falls under in context to the choices
available in the Explore Awesome Websites section. (ecommerce,personal,portfolio,magazine
,blog,business,entertainment.).
*In the project title you are required to fill your project's name,afterwhich you will be required to upload an image
of your site's landingpage,then alittle about your project in the project description and finally paste the link to the
live site or github repo in the link section.

Giving Ratings

*To give ratings to a website after review you are required to create an account
*Depending on your perception of the project you give an award on a scale of one to ten stars,
ten being excellent and one being unsatisfactory.
*Rating in terms of Usability means you award in context to how easy the interaction with the program or website was.
*Rating in terms of Design means you award in context to the outlook of the website.
*Rating in terms of Content means you award in context to useful the website's content is in terms of problem solving.

Updating My Profile

*To update your profile you can either navigate to the profile settings using the profile button on the navbar dropdown
Or the profile button on the sidebar

Password Change

*To reset your password you need to use the link in the login page where
you will be directed appropriately on how to change your password.

Web Search

*To search for posted wesites you need to have an account
*After creating an account a search bar will appear at the navbar, which you'll use to look up existing projects.
